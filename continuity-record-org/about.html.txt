        <h1>INSTITUTIONAL STATEMENT</h1>
        
        <p>
            The Public Coherence Archive is an independent, non-classified clearinghouse established to maintain structural transparency regarding localized physical, environmental, and informational discrepancies. 
        </p>
        
        <h2>MISSION MANDATE</h2>
        <p>
            When baseline parameters fluctuate within populated zones, public safety requires an objective record unhampered by institutional panic. Our function is documentation, cataloging, and structural preservation. We do not offer solutions; we ensure the data survives the shift.
        </p>
